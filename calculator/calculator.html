RPN Calculator

How to use:

    Enter values to be evaluated as well as operators separately in the input box then click enter to evaluate.
    i.e after each value, press "enter" or click anywhere outside of the box

    Example:

  • Type 5 and press enter
  • Type 6 and press enter
  • Type + and press enter
  • Then tap on the Evaluate button to get a result of 11
